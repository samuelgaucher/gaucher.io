Cookies

Cookies are small files a website can store on your computer to remember details about your browsing session. In Europe, website owners are required to obtain explicit consent from the users before setting any cookies, and to offer the possibility to opt-out from all the non-essential ones.

If you refuse incoming cookies, this website will minimally set one: one in which your privacy preference is stored, otherwise the banner would keep popping on every single page! Annoying.

I choose to use cookies to have an overview of the traffic on this website. This is done by gathering anonymous user information: approximate geographic location, device (mobile, desktop), new or returning, etc. I do this out of pure curiosity.
